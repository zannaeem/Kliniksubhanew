ABOUT KLINIK SUBHA

Our Background

Jash Pillay Medresources Sdn Bhd is a Sabah-based healthcare group, established in 2018 and headquartered in Beaufort. Our mission is to deliver accessible, high-quality, and holistic medical services, especially to underserved and semi-rural communities in Sabah.

Klinik Subha focuses on family-centered care, combining medical and physiotherapy services under one roof. We are committed to long-term patient relationships, offering comprehensive healthcare solutions for every stage of life.
