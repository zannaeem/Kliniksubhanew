Starting your journey in higher education? Our Student Health Screening package is designed to meet the medical requirements for entry into local public (IPTA) and private (IPTS) institutions. Get your check-up done quickly and efficiently.

Package Includes
  • Basic Health Examination by a Doctor
  • Weight, Height, and BMI Measurement
  • Eye Examination
  • Physical Examination
  • Urine Test (FEME)
  • Chest X-ray with Doctor's Report
Optional Add-ons
  • 5-in-1 Urine Drug Test: RM50
  • Hepatitis B / HIV Test: RM30
  • Full Blood Count (FBC): RM30
  • VDRL Test: RM20
