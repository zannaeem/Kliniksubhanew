About Klinik Subha

Who We Are

Jash Pillay Medresources Sdn Bhd is a dynamic healthcare group based in Sabah, founded on 17th August 2018 and headquartered in Beaufort. Our mission? To make quality, holistic healthcare accessible to everyone—especially in semi-rural and underserved communities.

We believe healthcare should be family-centered, personal, and continuous. That’s why we offer a full spectrum of services: from primary care and minor surgeries to pain management, physiotherapy, and rehabilitation. Every patient is unique, and we’re here for you at every stage of life.

Our Clinics & Centers
  1. Klinik Subha (Beaufort)
    Established in 1990 by Dr. Subhagan Chandran, this is Beaufort’s oldest general practitioner clinic. Now managed by Dr. Jason Jimmy Lee Pillay, we provide comprehensive outpatient care—consultations, screenings, minor procedures, and chronic disease management.
  2. Klinik Subha (Membakut)
    Opened in 2025, this branch brings affordable, reliable healthcare to Membakut and its surrounding areas, upholding the same high standards as our flagship clinic.
  3. Kinabalu Medic Spine & Rehabilitation Centre (Beaufort)
    Located in Bandar Mingo, this center specializes in physiotherapy, spine care, and rehabilitation—equipped with advanced therapy technology for sports injuries, post-surgical recovery, and chronic pain.

Our Commitment

At Jash Pillay Medresources Sdn Bhd, we are guided by the FAMILY CARE CONCEPT—delivering holistic, accessible, and personalized healthcare. We build long-term patient relationships by providing comprehensive medical and physiotherapy services under one roof, supporting every stage of life from general consultations to chronic pain management, rehabilitation, and preventive care.

Our commitment extends beyond clinical care. We actively engage in community health programs, wellness education, and corporate healthcare partnerships, contributing to the overall well-being of Sabah’s communities.
