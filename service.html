Our Services

Comprehensive Healthcare Solutions

Discover our wide range of medical services designed to meet all your healthcare needs

Student Health Screening (IPTA/IPTS)

Starting your journey in higher education? Our Student Health Screening package is designed to meet the medical requirements for entry into local public (IPTA) and private (IPTS) institutions.
Student Health Screening (School)

Ensure your child is healthy and ready for their academic year with our basic Student Health Screening. This package is ideal for school-going students.
GDL / PSV Health Screening (New Application)

Applying for a new Goods Driving License (GDL) or Public Service Vehicle (PSV) license? Our screening package covers all the necessary medical checks required by JPJ.
Full Deluxe Health Screening (Men)

Take a proactive approach to your health with our Full Deluxe screening for men, highly recommended for those aged 40 and above. Includes cancer markers.
Standard Health Screening (Most Popular)

Our most popular package offers a comprehensive overview of your health. Perfect for an annual check-up, helping you monitor vital health indicators.
Basic Pre-Employment Health Screening

Starting a new job? Our Basic Pre-Employment screening covers the essential health checks required by most employers.
Full Deluxe Health Screening (Women)

Designed specifically for women, our Full Deluxe screening offers a deep dive into your health. Recommended for women aged 40 and up.
GDL / PSV Health Screening (License Renewal)

Time to renew your GDL or PSV license? Our health screening package is tailored for a fast and hassle-free renewal process.
Basic Health Screening (Most Popular)

Looking for a great starting point to check on your health? Our popular Basic Health Screening provides essential insights by checking your blood and sugar levels.
Comprehensive Pre-Employment Health Screening

For job roles requiring a more detailed medical evaluation, our Comprehensive Pre-Employment package is the perfect fit.
Gardasil 9 Vaccine (HPV Prevention)

Protect yourself from the Human Papillomavirus (HPV), the leading cause of cervical cancer. Highly recommended for girls and women.
Meningococcal Vaccine (Haji & Umrah)

Performing your pilgrimage soon? The Meningococcal vaccine is a mandatory requirement for all Haji and Umrah pilgrims.
Typhoid Vaccine (For Food Handlers)

As a food handler, your health is crucial for public safety. This Typhoid vaccine is a mandatory requirement by the Ministry of Health Malaysia.
Influenza (Flu) Vaccine

The annual flu vaccine is your most effective defense against the influenza virus. Especially important for high-risk groups.
Pneumococcal Vaccine (Synflorix)

Protect your little ones from severe pneumococcal infections with the Synflorix vaccine. Helps prevent pneumonia and meningitis.
Rotavirus Vaccine

Rotavirus is a primary cause of severe gastroenteritis in infants and young children. Protect your child with this safe and effective vaccine.
Implanon Insertion (Family Planning)

Looking for a reliable, long-term family planning solution? Implanon provides continuous protection for up to three years with 99%+ effectiveness.
